Ratan Tata

By ABCD

Price: $29.99

Ratan Tata was a legendary Indian industrialist, visionary leader, and deeply revered philanthropist who served as the chairman of the Tata Group from 1991 to 2012. Stepping into leadership during India's economic liberalization, he boldly globalized the conglomerate by pulling off massive international acquisitions like Jaguar Land Rover, Tetley Tea, and Corus Steel. Beyond corporate milestones and his quest to build the world's most affordable car—the Tata Nano—his real legacy lies in his profound humility and ethics. For decades, he quietly directed over 60% of his wealth toward healthcare, education, and animal welfare through the Tata Trusts, earning India's highest civilian honors, the Padma Bhushan and Padma Vibhushan.

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's stone

By J K Rowling

Description:Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone (released as the Sorcerer's Stone in the US) is J.K. Rowling's 1997 fantasy novel that introduces Harry Potter, an orphaned boy mistreated by his cruel aunt and uncle. On his eleventh birthday, Harry learns he is actually a wizard and has been accepted into Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. He discovers that his parents were killed by the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, and that Harry himself miraculously survived the attack, leaving him famous in the wizarding world. Alongside his new best friends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, Harry navigates magical classes, uncovers a hidden plot involving a legendary magical object, and ultimately thwarts a dark conspiracy to restore Voldemort to power.

Untold story of MSD

By ABCD

Price: $82.00

Description:M.S. Dhoni: The Untold Story is a 2016 biographical sports film starring Sushant Singh Rajput that tracks the inspiring rise of India's legendary cricket captain from his humble beginnings in Ranchi. The movie vividly captures his early struggles as a ticket collector for the Indian Railways, his persistence through career stagnation and financial hurdles, and the deep personal losses he faced along the way. Supported by loyal friends and family, Dhoni defies the odds to debut for the national team, culminating in his historic, match-winning six at the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup final.

The famous Five (16)

By enid blyton

Description:The sixteenth book in Enid Blyton's Famous Five series is titled Five Go to Billycock Hill, published in 1957. In this adventure, Julian, Dick, Anne, George, and Timmy the dog go on a camping holiday at Billycock Hill near a farm owned by Julian and Dick's school friend, Toby. The peaceful holiday takes a dramatic turn when Toby’s cousin Jeff, a Royal Air Force pilot stationed at a nearby secret airfield, suddenly vanishes along with another pilot and two top-secret experimental aeroplanes. The media and authorities accuse Jeff of being a spy and a traitor who defected abroad, but the children refuse to believe he is guilty. Guided by a peculiar local butterfly farm, suspicious characters, and a message brought by a pet piglet named Curly, the Famous Five investigate the mystery.

The Secret Garden

By Frances Hodgson Burnett

Price:$84.00

Description:The Secret Garden is a classic 1911 children's novel by Frances Hodgson Burnett that tells the transformative story of Mary Lennox, a spoiled and unloved orphan sent from India to live at her uncle’s gloomy Yorkshire manor. Initially sour and lonely, Mary's life changes when she discovers a hidden, locked garden on the estate grounds and resolves to bring it back to life. Her secret project expands when she discovers Colin, her uncle's bedridden, hidden cousin who has been led to believe he is a hopeless invalid.
